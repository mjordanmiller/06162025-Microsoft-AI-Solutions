The Surface Pro 9 base model weighs 1.94lbs (879g). Additional features may add weight.
The Surface Pro 9 has a 13" edge-to-edge PixelSense touchscreen.
The Surface Pro 9 base model has an Intel 12th Gen processor and up to 15.5 hours of battery life.
The Surface Pro 9 with 5G and Microsoft SQ has up to 19 hours of battery life.
The Surface Pro has 2 USB-C ports to charge accessories, dock to high powered displays, or transfer files with ease.
